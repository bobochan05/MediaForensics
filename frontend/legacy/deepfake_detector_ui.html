01How the pipeline works
C1
CLIP
openai/clip-vit-base-patch32
Zero-shot scoring. Compares your image against two prompts — "a real photograph" and "an AI-generated image" — using broad pre-trained knowledge. Sensitive to style and semantic plausibility.
C2
EfficientNet
efficientnet_b0 · fine-tuned
ImageNet backbone fine-tuned on real vs. fake pairs. Detects low-level texture artefacts: unnatural pores, repeated patterns, and over-smoothed edges typical of generative models.
C3
DINO
dinov2_vits14 · fine-tuned
Vision transformer with dropout + cosine LR scheduling. Captures structural inconsistencies — facial geometry errors, impossible lighting, incoherent backgrounds CNN models miss.
Fusion MLP
clip_score+ eff_score+ dino_score→ fake_probability→ REAL · FAKE
