Layer 2 Summary (Click to know more...)

Layer 2 extends detection with multimodal source matching, external discovery, timeline estimation, and risk scoring. It explains where similar content appears and how confidently it relates to the uploaded media.

Layer 3 Summary (Click to know more...)

Layer 3 tracks propagation using growth rate, velocity, domain trust weighting, and risk status classification (stable, slow spread, spreading, viral). It powers alerting and long-term spread intelligence.
